# Things to Do on Hilton Head Island

Beyond its [pristine beaches](/guides/hilton-head-beaches/) and [world-class golf courses](/guides/hilton-head-golf/), Hilton Head Island offers countless activities for every age and interest. From dolphin encounters to Lowcountry cuisine, here's your complete guide.

## Top Attractions

### Harbour Town at Sea Pines

The heart of Hilton Head Island, Harbour Town features the iconic red and white striped lighthouse, a working marina, boutique shops, and waterfront restaurants.

**Must-Do:**
- Climb the Harbour Town Lighthouse for panoramic views
- Browse the shops and galleries
- Dine waterfront at sunset
- Watch boats and dolphins from the marina

**Access:** Within Sea Pines Resort ($8 gate fee for non-guests)

---

### Coastal Discovery Museum

Learn about Lowcountry history, ecology, and Gullah culture at this free museum featuring indoor exhibits and outdoor nature trails.

**Highlights:**
- Interactive exhibits on island history
- Honey Horn plantation grounds
- Nature trails and gardens
- Educational programs for kids

**Admission:** Free

---

### Pinckney Island National Wildlife Refuge

Just minutes from Hilton Head, this 4,053-acre refuge offers 14+ miles of trails through pristine Lowcountry habitat.

**Wildlife to Spot:**
- Alligators
- White-tailed deer
- Wading birds
- Bald eagles

**Best Time:** Early morning or late afternoon

**Admission:** Free

---

## Water Activities

### Dolphin Cruises

Bottlenose dolphins are abundant in Hilton Head's waters. Cruises depart from Harbour Town, Shelter Cove, and Broad Creek marinas.

**Types of Cruises:**
- Dolphin watching tours
- Sunset cruises
- Private charters
- Sailing adventures

---

### Kayaking and Paddleboarding

Explore the tidal creeks, marshes, and lagoons that surround Hilton Head Island.

**Popular Launch Points:**
- Palmetto Dunes lagoon system (11 miles)
- Broad Creek Marina
- Jarvis Creek
- Pinckney Island

**Rentals:** Available throughout the island

---

### Fishing

Hilton Head offers exceptional fishing opportunities:

**Inshore:** Redfish, flounder, spotted seatrout

**Offshore:** Mahi-mahi, wahoo, king mackerel

**Surf fishing:** Permitted on all beaches (license required for 16+)

---

### Boat Tours

**Popular Options:**
- Daufuskie Island ferry and tour
- Nature and eco-tours
- Crabbing excursions
- Private boat charters

---

## Land Activities

### Biking

Hilton Head is one of America's most bike-friendly destinations with over **60 miles of paved pathways**.

**Popular Routes:**
- Beach biking at low tide
- Sea Pines Forest Preserve
- Palmetto Dunes trails
- Cross-island pathway

**Rentals:** Available at most resorts and numerous shops

---

### Tennis

Ranked among the world's top tennis destinations, Hilton Head features:

- **Van Der Meer Tennis Academy** at Shipyard
- **Sea Pines Racquet Club**
- **Palmetto Dunes Tennis Center**
- **Port Royal Tennis**

---

### Zip Line Tours

**Zip Line Hilton Head** at Broad Creek offers an exhilarating canopy tour through the maritime forest.

---

### Horseback Riding

**Lawton Stables** in Sea Pines offers beach rides, trail rides, and pony rides for children.

---

## Family Activities

### Mini Golf

Multiple themed courses across the island:
- Pirate's Island Adventure Golf
- Adventure Cove
- Island Recreation Center

### Playground and Parks

- **Shelter Cove Community Park** - Playground, splash pad, waterfront
- **Driessen Beach Park** - Beach playground
- **Chaplin Community Park** - Sports facilities

### Nature Programs

- **Sea Turtle patrol** (summer, early morning)
- **Audubon bird walks**
- **Coastal Discovery Museum kids programs**
- **Nature excursions at resorts**

## Dining Experiences

### Seafood

Hilton Head's Lowcountry seafood is legendary:

- Fresh-off-the-boat shrimp
- She-crab soup
- Oyster roasts (fall/winter)
- Soft shell crab (spring)

### Dining Areas

**Harbour Town** - Upscale waterfront dining

**Coligny Plaza** - Casual beach dining

**Skull Creek** - Marina dining with sunset views

**Bluffton** - Off-island dining with Lowcountry charm

---

## Shopping

### Coligny Plaza
The island's main shopping district with boutiques, restaurants, and beach shops near Coligny Beach.

### Harbour Town
Upscale boutiques, galleries, and specialty shops within Sea Pines.

### Shelter Cove Harbour
Waterfront shopping, dining, and marina views.

### Tanger Outlets
Just off-island in Bluffton, featuring major brand outlets.

---

## Day Trips

### Daufuskie Island

Take the ferry from Hilton Head to this car-free island with Gullah history, beautiful beaches, and golf courses.

### Savannah, Georgia

45 minutes away, explore historic squares, museums, and renowned restaurants.

### Beaufort, South Carolina

30 minutes away, this charming town offers antebellum architecture and waterfront dining.

- **Jazz festivals**

- **Wine festivals**

## Plan Your Itinerary

**3-Day Beach Vacation:**
- Day 1: Beach time at Coligny, Harbour Town sunset
- Day 2: Morning dolphin cruise, afternoon biking, dinner at Skull Creek
- Day 3: Kayaking, shopping, beach time

**5-Day Golf & Beach:**
- Day 1: Arrive, beach orientation
- Day 2: Morning golf, afternoon beach
- Day 3: Dolphin cruise, Harbour Town, dinner
- Day 4: Golf round two, kayaking
- Day 5: Beach, departure

**Family Week:**
- Mix beach days with dolphin cruises, biking, mini golf, and nature programs
