# Hilton Head Island Vacation Rentals Guide

Hilton Head Island offers over **10,000 vacation rental properties**, from oceanfront condos to luxury villas in gated plantation communities. With an average occupancy rate of 59% and nightly rates averaging $480, finding the perfect Lowcountry retreat requires knowing the island's distinct areas.

## Why Choose a Vacation Rental?

Vacation rentals on Hilton Head Island offer significant advantages:

- **More space** - Full kitchens, living areas, multiple bedrooms
- **Better value** - Often less per-person than hotels
- **Location variety** - Oceanfront, golf course, or marsh views
- **Privacy** - Your own home away from home
- **Local experience** - Live like an islander

## Popular Rental Areas

### Sea Pines Resort

**The premier Hilton Head destination**

Sea Pines is Hilton Head's most prestigious address, a 5,000-acre gated community home to Harbour Town and world-class golf.

**Property Types:**
- Luxury villas and townhomes
- Oceanfront and golf course homes
- Harbour Town Marina properties

**Highlights:**
- Three championship golf courses including Harbour Town
- Private beaches and beach club
- Harbour Town with shops and restaurants
- Iconic lighthouse
- Extensive bike trails

**Price Range:** $$$ - $$$$

**Best for:** Golf vacations, luxury seekers, special occasions

**Note:** $8 gate fee for non-guests

---

### Palmetto Dunes Oceanfront Resort

**Full-service resort experience**

Palmetto Dunes is a 2,000-acre oceanfront resort offering the best combination of beaches, golf, tennis, and family activities.

**Property Types:**
- Oceanfront condos and villas
- Golf course properties
- Lagoon-front homes

**Highlights:**
- 3 miles of private beach
- 11-mile lagoon system for kayaking
- Three championship golf courses
- World-class tennis center
- On-site dining options

**Price Range:** $$ - $$$$

**Best for:** Families, active vacationers, water sports enthusiasts

---

### Shipyard Plantation

**Central location with resort amenities**

Shipyard offers a mid-island location with easy access to beaches, golf, and island attractions.

**Property Types:**
- Condos and townhomes
- Golf villa properties
- Beach-accessible rentals

**Highlights:**
- 27 holes of golf
- Beach access
- Tennis courts
- Van Der Meer Tennis Academy
- Sonesta Resort on property

**Price Range:** $$ - $$$

**Best for:** Families seeking value, tennis players, central location

---

### Forest Beach

**Steps from Coligny Beach**

Forest Beach is an unincorporated area adjacent to Coligny Beach, offering the most convenient beach access on the island.

**Property Types:**
- Beach houses and cottages
- Condo complexes
- Oceanfront properties

**Highlights:**
- Walking distance to Coligny Beach (free parking)
- Coligny Plaza shops and restaurants
- No gate fees
- Lively atmosphere

**Price Range:** $ - $$$

**Best for:** Beach lovers, nightlife seekers, budget-conscious travelers

---

## Property Types Explained

### Oceanfront Condos
Direct beach access, typically in high-rise or low-rise buildings. Best views and convenience but higher prices.

### Villas
Attached or semi-detached properties often in resort communities. Good balance of space, amenities, and price.

### Beach Houses
Single-family homes near the beach. Maximum privacy and space for large groups.

### Golf Course Properties
Views of fairways and greens. Often include golf packages. Peaceful settings.

### Lagoon/Marsh Views
Properties overlooking Hilton Head's tidal marshes. Wildlife viewing, often better value.

## What to Look for in a Rental

### Must-Have Amenities
- Air conditioning (essential May-September)
- Full kitchen
- Washer/dryer
- WiFi
- Beach chairs and umbrella
- Bikes (common in resort communities)

### Nice-to-Have Features
- Private pool
- Hot tub
- Ocean or golf views
- Screened porch
- Garage parking

### Questions to Ask
1. Is linens/towels included?
2. What is the check-in/check-out process?
3. Are there additional fees (cleaning, resort, parking)?
4. Is the property in a gated community?
5. What is the cancellation policy?

## Tips for Booking

1. **Book early for peak season** - Summer weeks book 3-6 months ahead
2. **Check total costs** - Add cleaning fees, taxes, and resort fees
3. **Read reviews carefully** - Recent reviews matter most
4. **Verify beach access** - Not all properties have direct access
5. **Consider gate fees** - Sea Pines and Port Royal charge daily gate fees
6. **Look for golf packages** - Often included with resort rentals

## Getting Around from Your Rental

Most visitors rent a car, but Hilton Head is also bike-friendly:

- **60+ miles of bike paths** throughout the island
- **Beach biking** at low tide
- **Free trolley** service in summer
- **Ride share** available (Uber, Lyft)
