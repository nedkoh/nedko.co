Hilton Head Island offers the perfect blend of natural beauty, outdoor adventure, and Lowcountry charm. With 2.8 million visitors annually, this barrier island in South Carolina has earned its reputation as one of the top family beach destinations in the country.

## Hilton Head Island Beaches

Hilton Head's beaches are consistently ranked among the best in the United States. All beaches offer firm, packable sand ideal for biking and building sandcastles.

### Top Beach Destinations

- **Coligny Beach Park** - The island's most popular beach with free parking, restrooms, and nearby shopping at Coligny Plaza
- **Burkes Beach** - Quieter atmosphere perfect for shelling and nature walks
- **Driessen Beach Park** - Family favorite featuring a playground and covered pavilion
- **Folly Field Beach** - Excellent for sandcastles with flat, firm sand
- **Mitchelville Beach** - Peaceful, rustic setting ideal for nature lovers
- **South Beach** - Wide open stretches and spectacular views near Sea Pines

## Golf on Hilton Head Island

Hilton Head Island is a golfer's paradise, featuring 24 championship courses within just 12 miles of coastline.

### Premier Golf Courses

- **Harbour Town Golf Links** - Home to the PGA TOUR RBC Heritage since 1969, featuring the iconic lighthouse
- **Atlantic Dunes at Sea Pines** - Complete reconstruction of the island's first golf course
- **Arthur Hills at Palmetto Dunes** - Ranked among South Carolina's top 30 playable courses
- **Robert Trent Jones at Palmetto Dunes** - Classic design with lagoons and ocean views
- **Oyster Reef Golf Club** - Semi-private favorite with stunning marsh views
- **Palmetto Hall Plantation** - 36 holes designed by Arthur Hills and Robert Cupp

## Vacation Rentals & Accommodations

Find the perfect place to stay on Hilton Head Island, from beachfront condos to spacious family homes in gated plantation communities.

### Popular Rental Areas

- **Sea Pines Resort** - Gated community with Harbour Town, beaches, and multiple golf courses
- **Palmetto Dunes** - 2,000-acre oceanfront resort with golf, tennis, and lagoon system
- **Shipyard Plantation** - Central location with beach access and championship golf
- **Port Royal** - Quiet elegance with three golf courses and private beaches
- **Forest Beach** - Steps from Coligny Beach and the island's best restaurants

## Getting to Hilton Head Island

- **Savannah/Hilton Head International Airport (SAV)** - 45 minutes from the island
- **Hilton Head Island Airport (HXD)** - On-island for private aircraft
- **By Car** - I-95 to Exit 8 (US 278), follow signs to Hilton Head Island
